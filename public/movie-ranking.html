The Best Movies According to Rajeev

My top 3 movies of all time.

Lagaan

It is based on real story of Bhuvan, group of people learning a new game (Cricket) for purpose.

Dilwale Dulhania Le Jaayenge

Heart touching love story of college going boy and girl from different family background.

3 Idiots

Nice comedy and drama while highlighting education system demerits and suggestions for improvement.
